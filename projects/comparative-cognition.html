Lung Transplant Decision Aid

A human-computer interaction capstone project focused on designing a clinical decision support tool for patients evaluating lung transplant options.

Overview

This project involved user research, interface prototyping, and iterative testing to develop a decision aid that helped patients weigh risk, quality of life outcomes, and survival projections.

Methods

  • User interviews
  • Workflow mapping
  • Interface prototyping
  • Iterative usability testing
