Research, statistical modeling, and data-driven systems.

I design studies that aim to answer practical questions in complex, real-world environments. My work combines careful experimental design with statistical modeling to make sense of high-variance data and to support decisions that extend beyond the lab.

I am particularly interested in how organisms and systems behave when conditions are not perfect. If signals are noisy or degraded, how can we still draw rigorous and applicable conclusions?
