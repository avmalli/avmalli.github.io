Research, evaluation, and data-driven systems.

I am a research scientist working at the intersection of behavioral science, statistical modeling, and applied data analysis. My work focuses on designing empirical evaluations, analyzing complex and high-variance datasets, and building reproducible analytical workflows that support clear, defensible conclusions in real-world settings.

I am particularly interested in how systems learn, generalize, and fail under uncertainty, and in developing evaluation frameworks that balance rigor, interpretability, and practical impact.
