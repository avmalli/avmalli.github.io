Mallikarjun, A., Shroads, E., & Newman, R. S. (2024). Perception of vocoded speech in domestic dogs. Animal Cognition, 27(1), 34.

Controlled evaluation of performance under signal degradation to isolate what information is necessary for recognition. Emphasizes robustness, failure modes, and generalization when input quality is systematically degraded.

Mallikarjun, A., Collins, A., Verta, A., Hanadari-Levy, A., Chaskes, M. B., Rosen, M. R., ... & Otto, C. M. (2023). Canine scent detection of sinonasal-inverted papilloma in blood plasma and nasal secretions. Journal of Veterinary Behavior, 62, 29-38.

End-to-end translational evaluation in a clinically relevant setting, integrating study design, data constraints, and performance analysis. Highlights rigor under real-world operational conditions.

DOI PDF Project

Tsui, A. S. M., Erickson, L. C., Mallikarjun, A., Thiessen, E. D., & Fennell, C. T. (2021). Dual language statistical word segmentation in infancy: Simulating a language-mixing bilingual environment. Developmental Science, 24(3), e13050.

Empirical and computational framing of learning in mixed, high-variance inputs. Addresses generalization when training and test distributions are not cleanly separated.

DOI PDF Project

Mallikarjun, A., Shroads, E., & Newman, R. S. (2019). The cocktail party effect in the domestic dog (Canis familiaris). Animal Cognition, 22(3), 423-432.

Study of recognition performance under competing auditory interference, isolating factors that support or degrade selective attention in realistic environments.

DOI PDF Project

Mallikarjun, A., Newman, R. S., & Novick, J. M. (2017). Exploiting the interconnected lexicon: Bootstrapping English language learning in young Spanish speakers. Translational Issues in Psychological Science, 3(1), 34.

Review and synthesis comparing psycholinguistic evidence on bilingual language learning with prevailing pedagogical assumptions. Bridges empirical research and educational practice, highlighting where evidence supports or challenges common instructional models.
